Market-Defining News/Developments

  • China/Asia Macro

  • Energy & Commodities

  • Corporate/Sector

    • Mega-cap tech earnings and guidance will be closely watched amid demand uncertainty, macro headwinds (US & Asia), and AI infrastructure cost inflation.

    • 2Y and 10Y Treasury yields both volatile but off highs; any further oil shock could invert the recent steepening, re-pricing recession odds.
