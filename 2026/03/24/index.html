Summary Investment Implications

  • Quality bias: Macro environment supportive to “quality” large caps, especially tech and energy, as financial conditions remain loose but tail risks (inflation/oil, geopolitics/shutdown, DHS funding) keep volatility up.
  • Cyclicals/industrials: Directional plays subject to swings on every oil/energy headline and CPI print; risk-reward no longer as strong as Q4/Q1.
  • Energy: Short gamma—headline driven; own direct exposure for crisis tail risk, but size for mean reversion if ceasefire/negotiations stick.
  • Rates/duration: Treasury curve risk high; upward moves in long yields driven by term premium (oil/inflation/fiscal). Defensive rate and TIPS–linked plays favored short-term, but second-order yield curve normalization delayed.
  • Banks: Large cap earnings should be neutral, but lean defensive; watch credit.
  • Crypto: Continue overweight BTC/ETH short-term; take profits into spikes on new policy headlines, but don’t discount potential for “true” safe-haven bid if Gulf/Mideast situation tanks or US/ICE crack down hard.
