Macro

US and Iran diplomatic channels open, but standoff is unresolved. Any breakthrough on ceasefire talks or partial reopening could see sharp oil/gold reversal; new risk premium priced into energy, defense, shipping, and commodities.

  • Fed pricing: April FOMC (end of month) and data in focus—expect no cut. Market not pricing imminent cut, especially with supply shocks and ongoing labor tightness. Recent FRED (jobs, claims) show only very modest softening; no credit crunch visible.
  • Credit: No immediate stress in US bank system, but widening high yield/IG spreads; attention to bank conference calls for recession language, energy lending and commercial RE lending/covenant strains.
  • Retail sales and jobs: March FRED numbers climb, but sentiment somewhat down; jobs/growth softening risk exists but not acute yet.
